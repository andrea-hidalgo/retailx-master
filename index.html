Our Retail Network

We move the next generation of great product startup companies to scale with major retailers

The challenge facing hardware innovators: how do you cut through the noise of today’s crowded ecosystem and get the attention of the the global retail giants that can get your product into customers’ hands?

RetailXelerator is the only accelerator that bridges the gap between product companies and retailers. We give startups the tools, resources and mentorship to get their products and their businesses retail-ready - and then we put them in front of the major retailers to make the pitch.

We take companies from startup to scaleup.

As a RetailXelerator portfolio company, you’ll spend 12 weeks in a virtual course, learning the ins and outs of retail from industry heavyweights who have hands-on experience growing products for billion-dollar retailers.

Along the way, we’ll connect you with capability partners, who will ensure your product is ready for retail shelves.

At the end of it all: Retailer Portfolio Day. We put you in front of decision makers from the world’s biggest retail giants, so you can tell them why your product should be on their shelves – and show them that you’re ready to keep pace with them.
